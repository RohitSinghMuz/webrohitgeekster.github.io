MRG The Balcony sector 93 Gurgaon



MRG the Balcony sector 93 Gurgaon is the first affordable property launched by the MRG World. It is a near to possession project. Developed as per the guidelines of Affordable Housing Policy 2013, this residential avenue comes at a reasonable price. This architectural structure occupies the land area of 5 acres. As a residential complex, it inhabits approximately 731 inventories which are curated with modern fixtures. To make your living comfortable and serene, we have built some exclusive amenities. MRG The Meridian sector 89 Gurgaon



MRG The Meridian sector 89 Gurgaon is the second affordable housing project by them . The project standing closest to Dwarka Expressway is well linked with the same.
